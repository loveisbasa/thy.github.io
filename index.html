Haoyue Tang

 

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a postdoctoral associate in at Meta AI. Previously I was a postdoc at the Institute of Network Science, Yale University, working with Prof. Leandros Tassiulas.

I received my PhD and my Bachelor degrees in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in 2022 and 2017, respectively. My advisor is Prof. Jintao Wang. During my PhD studies, I was a visiting student in Telecom Paris, Microsoft Research Asia and Tencent Inc.

Research Interests

  • Performance Modeling and Optimization of Networking Systems (6G Wireless Networks, Machine Learning Systems)

  • Online and Reinforcement Learning in Wireless Systems

  • Theory and Applications of Generative Models (Diffusion Models) My research work have been published in NeurIPS, IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking (ToN), IEEE Transactions on Information Theory, IEEE Infocom and ACM Mobihoc.
